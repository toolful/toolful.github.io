How to Take the Typing Speed Test

Follow these simple steps to measure your typing speed:

  • Start: Click on the text area to begin typing. The timer starts automatically.
  • Type: Type the highlighted text exactly as shown. Blue indicates correct letters, Red indicates errors.
  • Result: After 60 seconds, your WPM (Words Per Minute) and Accuracy percentage will be displayed.
  • Improve: Use the "Try Again" button to practice and improve your score.

What is WPM?

WPM (Words Per Minute) is the standard metric for measuring typing speed. It is calculated by dividing the total number of characters typed by 5 (the standardized length of a word) and then dividing that number by the minutes spent typing. A higher WPM indicates a faster typing speed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I take a typing test?

Taking a typing test helps benchmark your current skills. Regular practice can improve muscle memory, reduce finger fatigue, and increase productivity for students and professionals.

Is this typing test mobile-friendly?

Yes, the Toolful Typing Speed Test is 100% responsive and optimized for mobile devices, tablets, and desktops.

How is accuracy calculated?

Accuracy is the ratio of correctly typed characters to total characters typed, expressed as a percentage. High speed with low accuracy will result in a lower effective score.
