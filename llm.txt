Toolful AI (https://toolful.github.io/) is a free web platform offering browser-based AI productivity tools.

Main tools:

Wordify (https://toolful.github.io/wordify.html) – AI writing assistant for rewriting, paraphrasing, and humanizing text.

CV Generator (https://toolful.github.io/cv.html) – Creates ATS-friendly resumes for students and professionals.

QR Code Generator (https://toolful.github.io/qrcode.html) – Generates clean QR codes for business and personal use.

Image Converter (https://toolful.github.io/img.html) – Converts images to PDF, JPG, PNG, and text formats.

YouTube Banner Maker (https://toolful.github.io/youtube.html) – Resizes images for YouTube channel art.

You AI Detector (https://toolful.github.io/you.html) – Detects AI-generated text and improves human tone.
